A little bit about me.

I was a 6th grade science teacher for 6 years in NYC. My last 1.5 years of teaching was done remotely. While I taught online, my favorite tool to use was Minecraft Education Edition. I raised $600 to get the game deployed on all of my students' Chromebooks. As I used the game, I began to realize I throughouly enjoyed creating lessons and building worlds and using code in the game to do it. This is where my journey into tech started.

In September 2021, my husband and I decided to leave New York and start a life of travel. I knew that remote teaching wouldn't last forever, so I decided to follow my passion for creating and pursued front-end web development. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized just how many skills are transferable from teaching to tech.
